Description: Alternaria is a fungal disease that affects mango leaves, causing dark brown to black lesions with a concentric ring pattern. It's commonly found in humid regions and spreads through wind-blown spores.
Treatment: Apply fungicides, prune infected branches, and maintain proper spacing for airflow.
