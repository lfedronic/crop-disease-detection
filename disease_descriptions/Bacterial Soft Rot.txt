Description: Bacterial Soft Rot is a bacterial disease that affects cauliflower leaves, causing circular, water-soaked lesions. It spreads through rain splashes and irrigation.
Treatment: Remove infected leaves, apply copper-based sprays, and avoid overhead watering.
